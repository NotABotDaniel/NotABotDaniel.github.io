This journal entire details the game I am making for the final project this year.

What genre will your game be?

A 3D maze game. (puzzle game)

What are the objectives? Will there be a score? How do you win or lose?

Your goal is to get to the onjective at the end of the maze as fast as possible. There will be a timer. You cannot lose.

How will the player interact with the game?

The player can move around using arrow keys. Left/right for rotation and up/down for forward/backward

What features will you include in your MVP?

  • Arrow key controlls for the player
  • Maze with colision detection
  • 3D view window
  • Objective at the end of the maze
